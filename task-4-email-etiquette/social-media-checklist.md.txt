# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful profile  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Think before posting any content  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Keep your privacy settings updated  

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information  
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Avoid accepting requests from unknown people  
- Do not overshare your location or daily activities
