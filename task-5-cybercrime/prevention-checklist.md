# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media  
- Always verify the authenticity of websites before entering login details  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Never share personal information like OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone  

## 💰 Financial / UPI Safety
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — it is only required for sending money  
- Always verify UPI payment requests before approving them  
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources  

## 🌐 General Safety
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Regularly update your apps and device software for security  
- Log out from accounts when using shared devices  

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
